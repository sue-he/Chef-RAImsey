's Chocolate-Cookie-Crumbs Cake

This tasty cake uses chocolate cookie crumbs and heavy whipping cream, instead of royal icing to keep it

2 cup  chocolate cookie crumbs
½ cup whipped heavy whipping cream
1 and ½ cup warmed milk
1 cup divided cookie butter
3 teaspoon melted salt
4 cup peeled, cored, thinly sliced apples
2 teaspoon or to taste ground cinnamon
