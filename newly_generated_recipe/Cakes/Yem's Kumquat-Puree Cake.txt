Yem's Kumquat-Puree Cake

This tasty cake uses kumquat puree and lemon juice, known as this yummy pineapple cake uses kumquat puree and lemon

1  thawed (12 ounce) bag frozen pitted dark sweet cherries

4 ounce  sour cream
½ teaspoon or more to taste orange extract
