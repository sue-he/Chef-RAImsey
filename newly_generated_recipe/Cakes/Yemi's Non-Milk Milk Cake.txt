Yemi's Non-Milk Milk Cake

This tasty cake uses milk and graham crackers,  so maybe its not as naughty as it first

1 and ½ cup warmed milk
2 cup blended graham crackers
⅛ teaspoon softened salt
1 teaspoon  lemon
½ cup divided white sugar
1  room temperature large egg yolk
½ tablespoon softened unsalted butter
1 cup or as needed vegetable oil
1 and ½ tablespoon softened margarine
