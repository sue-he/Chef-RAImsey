Maanya's Almond_extract Cake

This tasty cake uses almond extract and butter, not icing sweet and rich, with chocolate cake slices

½ teaspoon  almond extract
¾ cup softened butter
1 and ½ tablespoon sifted all-purpose flour
3 tablespoon  margarine
2 cup packed brown sugar
1 and ½ teaspoon  lemon juice
4 cup rinsed, drained fresh blackberries
¾ teaspoon  baking powder
4  room temperature eggs
1 cup  semisweet chocolate chips
½ teaspoon cold water
