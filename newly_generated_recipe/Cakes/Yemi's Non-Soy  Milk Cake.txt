Yemi's Non-Soy  Milk Cake

This tasty cake uses milk and organic cane sugar, to cut down on any saturated fat, so it's a pretty heart-healthy

½ cup warmed milk
2 tablespoon refrigerated organic cane sugar
⅛ teaspoon room temperature salt
3 tablespoon  apricot jam
3 tablespoon grated lemon zest
1  beaten egg
3 pound pitted, sliced fresh peaches - peeled
1 teaspoon or to taste ground cinnamon
¼ cup divided water
2 and ½ cup  rolled oats
1 and ½ cup chopped raisins
1 and ½ cup toasted chopped pecans
