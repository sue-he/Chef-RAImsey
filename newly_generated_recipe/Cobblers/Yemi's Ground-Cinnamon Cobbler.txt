Yemi's Ground-Cinnamon Cobbler

This tasty cobbler uses ground cinnamon and mashed bananas, a sweet and creamy custard with rum and lemon in a graham cracker

1 pinch or to taste ground cinnamon
1 cup chopped mashed bananas
¾ cup divided white sugar
1 quart warmed chocolate milk
2 and ⅓ tablespoon sifted all-purpose flour
2 cup whipped heavy whipping cream
1  softened (8 ounce) package cream cheese
4 cup  shortening
