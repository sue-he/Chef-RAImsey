Yemi's Non-Pecans Milk Cobbler

This tasty cobbler uses milk and egg

5 tablespoon warmed milk
3  beaten egg yolks
¾ cup sifted white cake mix
1  thawed (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping
¼ cup  chocolate shavings
1  baked (9 inch) pie shell
1 and ½ tablespoon divided water
1  softened (8 ounce) package cream cheese
1 cup thawed frozen whipped topping
14 can warmed sweetened condensed milk
1 teaspoon  pumpkin pie spice
