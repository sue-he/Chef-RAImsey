Maanya's Walnuts Cobbler

This tasty cobbler uses walnuts and salt,  so there's no added fat or calories.

½ cup chopped walnuts
½ teaspoon  salt
3  room temperature eggs
½ cup  apricot brandy
1 cup  sour cream
1 cup cold water
¾ teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 cup  heavy whipping cream
1 tablespoon  lemon juice
1  room temperature egg
½ cup packed brown sugar
