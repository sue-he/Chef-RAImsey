Yemi's Self-Rising-Flour Cobbler

This tasty cobbler uses self-rising flour and white sugar, and is yummy with cream or ice cream! Yes, I’m addicted to cream

1 cup  self-rising flour
⅓ cup divided white sugar
1 cup  flaked coconut
¾ tablespoon sifted all-purpose flour
4 cup rinsed and drained fresh blackberries
¼ teaspoon room temperature salt
½ cup  raisins
2 tablespoon  water
