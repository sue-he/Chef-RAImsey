Yemi's Salt Pie

This tasty pie uses salt and dried apricots, no secret spices here! This post may contain affiliate

¼ teaspoon room temperature salt
⅓ cup chopped dried apricots
¾ tablespoon divided water
6 cup peeled, cored and sliced peeled and sliced apples
2 and ½ cup sifted all-purpose flour
3 tablespoon  hot water
3 cup  unsweetened applesauce
2 cup  red and green candied cherries
