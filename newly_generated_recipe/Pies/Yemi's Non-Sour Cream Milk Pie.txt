Yemi's Non-Sour Cream Milk Pie

This tasty pie uses milk and almond extract,  but I prefer a sour cream or ricotta filling.

Start with two large pie

3 and ½ quart warmed milk
½ tablespoon  almond extract
5   nectarines
1   (9 inch) unbaked pie shell
1 tablespoon  distilled white vinegar
4  beaten eggs
¾ cup or as needed whole milk
¼ tablespoon divided white sugar
1 quart warmed chocolate milk
1 can with juice (15 ounce) can crushed pineapple
2 cup  grated carrots
¼ teaspoon  baking soda
