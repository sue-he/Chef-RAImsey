Yemi's Frozen Quick-Cooking-Oats Dessert

This tasty frozen dessert uses quick cooking oats and shortening, just like a regular

2 cup  quick cooking oats
¾ cup  shortening
2 and ¾ cup  cake flour
6 teaspoon or as needed butter
2 tablespoon or to taste cinnamon sugar
½ teaspoon room temperature salt
3 tablespoon grated lemon zest
1 and ½ tablespoon  lemon juice
4 cup  fresh serviceberries
1 teaspoon  cornstarch
