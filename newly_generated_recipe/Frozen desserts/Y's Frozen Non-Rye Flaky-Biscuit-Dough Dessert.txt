Y's Frozen Non-Rye Flaky-Biscuit-Dough Dessert

This tasty frozen dessert uses flaky biscuit dough and all-purpose flour,  cinnamon, chocolate chips, and ice cream,

1 can refrigerated flaky biscuit dough
2 and ⅓ cup unsifted all-purpose flour
1   large egg
½   package almond paste
1 tablespoon packed brown sugar
½ cup  baking mix
¼ cup  flaked coconut
4 cup sifted confectioners' sugar
3 cup sliced and frozen strawberries
¼ teaspoon chilled and diced butter
1   (9 inch) unbaked pie shell
5   nectarines
1 cup cold heavy whipping cream
