's Frozen (9-Inch)-Unbaked-Pie-Crust Dessert

This tasty frozen dessert uses (9 inch) unbaked pie crust and corn

1   (9 inch) unbaked pie crust
2 tablespoon  corn syrup
3 tablespoon  bourbon whiskey
1  unbaked (9 inch) store-bought pie crust
1 and ½ teaspoon room temperature butter
1 tablespoon  cornstarch
2 cup packed brown sugar
2 cup or as needed confectioners' sugar
½ cup toasted chopped pecans
