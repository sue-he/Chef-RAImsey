Sue's Frozen Water Dessert

This tasty frozen dessert uses water and fresh blueberries, not heavy cream! sweet and refreshing real

1 and ¼ cup or as needed water
2 ounce  fresh blueberries
⅔ cup or as needed water
¾ tablespoon  shortening
¼ cup coarsely chopped chopped almonds
3 and ½ tablespoon sifted all-purpose flour
1   (9 inch) unbaked deep-dish pie crust
½ teaspoon  cream of tartar
2  baked (9 inch)  pie shells
1 and ½ cup warmed evaporated milk
3  slightly beaten large eggs
¾ cup refrigerated granulated sugar
3 tablespoon  baking soda
