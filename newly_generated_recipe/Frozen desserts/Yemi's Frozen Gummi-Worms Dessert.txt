Yemi's Frozen Gummi-Worms Dessert

This tasty frozen dessert uses gummi worms and graham cracker, cookie

10   gummi worms
1 cup baked graham cracker
1 cup melted semisweet chocolate chips
½ cup  unsweetened cocoa powder
1 and ¼ cup toasted chopped pecans
¾ teaspoon or as needed water
6  slightly beaten eggs
2 cup  lemon-lime flavored carbonated beverage
1 can crushed, drained pineapple
1 cup  shredded coconut
2 can warmed sweetened condensed milk
⅓ teaspoon chilled and diced butter
