Sue's Frozen Baking-Mix Dessert

This tasty frozen dessert uses baking mix and butter, some eggs, milk, and cool whip to create a sweet, crunchy

2 cup  baking mix
10 tablespoon melted butter
1   (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix
1 teaspoon or to taste vanilla
1 and ⅓ cup or as needed vegetable oil
3  room temperature eggs
1  thawed (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping
1 and ½ cup melted margarine
¾ tablespoon  orange juice
⅓ cup packed brown sugar
