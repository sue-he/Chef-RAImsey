S's Frozen Baking-Mix Dessert

This tasty frozen dessert uses baking mix and vegetable

2 cup  baking mix
⅓ cup or as needed vegetable oil
1 and ¼ tablespoon or to taste ground cinnamon
1 cup or as needed coconut oil
1 and ½ teaspoon  ground ginger
1 cup softened vanilla ice cream
1 and ½ cup softened margarine
½ cup sliced and frozen strawberries
1 and ½ tablespoon  baking powder
2 cup  powdered sugar
3  slightly beaten large eggs
