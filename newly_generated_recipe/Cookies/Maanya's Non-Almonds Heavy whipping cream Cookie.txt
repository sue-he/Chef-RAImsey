Maanya's Non-Almonds Heavy whipping cream Cookie

This tasty cookie uses heavy whipping cream and ,  sliced and pureed white chocolate

1 cup heavy whipping cream
4  peeled, cored, sliced 
1  softened (8 ounce) package cream cheese
¼ tablespoon cold water
½ teaspoon  almond extract
3  beaten eggs
1 cup  (12 fluid ounce) can evaporated milk
3 tablespoon sifted all-purpose flour
1  room temperature egg yolk
6 cup peeled, sliced apples
½ teaspoon grated lemon zest
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
