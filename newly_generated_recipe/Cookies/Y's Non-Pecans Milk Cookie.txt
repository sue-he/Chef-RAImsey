Y's Non-Pecans Milk Cookie

This tasty cookie uses milk and container frozen whipped topping,  which is really the only kind of whipped topping I can

3 and ½ cup warmed milk
1  thawed container frozen whipped topping
2 tablespoon warmed milk
1   (3.5 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix
1 cup  lemon juice
½ cup  plain yogurt
1 cup divided white sugar
½ cup or as needed whole milk
