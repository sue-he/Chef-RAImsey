Maanya's Strawberries Cookie

This tasty cookie uses strawberries and (12 fluid ounce) can evaporated milk, instead of white

2  thawed strawberries
1 quart  (12 fluid ounce) can evaporated milk
¾ cup divided white sugar
1 and ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
1 and ½ cup rolled oats
1 teaspoon  vanilla extract
1 cup sliced bananas
¾ cup  shortening
1  room temperature egg
½ teaspoon grated lemon zest
