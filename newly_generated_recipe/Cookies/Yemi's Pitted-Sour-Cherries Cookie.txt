Yemi's Pitted-Sour-Cherries Cookie

This tasty cookie uses pitted sour cherries and butter,  wrapped in almond shortbread. I’ve actually tried to make this recipe.

2 cup  pitted sour cherries
2 cup melted butter
1 and ½ tablespoon  vanilla extract
2 cup  fresh blueberries
1  thawed (8 ounce) container frozen whipped topping
½ cup  milk
3  room temperature eggs
1   (3.5 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix
1 and ¼ cup  blueberries
4 cup  heavy whipping cream
1 teaspoon  lemon juice
1 teaspoon  cornstarch
