Reese's Non-Cream cheese Blueberries Cookie

This tasty cookie uses blueberries and water,  but we can't get to those darn blueberries!

1 and ¼ cup  blueberries
¼ tablespoon boiling water
2 tablespoon  candy-coated milk chocolate pieces
1   (16 ounce) package prepared chocolate frosting
⅓ cup  vegetable oil
5  beaten eggs
1 cup flaked shredded coconut
½ cup  baking mix
3 tablespoon melted butter
1  softened (3 ounce) package cream cheese
2   (3.5 ounce) packages instant banana pudding mix
