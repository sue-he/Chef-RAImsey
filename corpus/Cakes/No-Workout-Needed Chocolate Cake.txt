No-Workout-Needed Chocolate Cake

This chocolate cake is warm, yummy, and guilt-free. No workout required to burn off those cake calories! 

1 teaspoon coconut oil, or as needed 
1 (15.25 ounce) package dark chocolate cake mix 
1 (12 ounce) package soft tofu 
½ cup brewed coffee 
¼ cup dark chocolate chips 
3 tablespoons almond milk 
2 tablespoons powdered peanut butter (such as PB2®)
