Fig Spice Cake

This is a delicious fig cake. 

1 pound dried figs 
½ cup buttermilk 
1 ½ cups all-purpose flour 
1 teaspoon baking powder 
½ teaspoon baking soda 
1 teaspoon salt

¼ teaspoon ground cloves 
½ cup butter 
1 cup white sugar 
2  eggs 
1 teaspoon vanilla extract 
1 cup chopped walnuts
