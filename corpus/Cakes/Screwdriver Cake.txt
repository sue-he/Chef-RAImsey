Screwdriver Cake

This recipe uses cake mix, pudding mix, orange juice, and vodka to make a fun, light cake with a kick. 

1 (18.25 ounce) package yellow cake mix 
1 (3.5 ounce) package instant vanilla pudding mix 
¾ cup orange juice 
½ cup vodka 
¼ cup vegetable oil 
3  eggs 
1 cup confectioners' sugar 
3 tablespoons orange juice
