Kelley's Peach Cobbler

Serve with whipped cream or vanilla ice cream to put this over-the-top! 

½ cup butter, melted 
1 (16 ounce) package frozen peach slices 
⅔ cup white sugar 
½ cup water 
1 cup all-purpose flour 
1 cup white sugar 
½ cup milk 
1 ½ teaspoons baking powder 
¼ teaspoon salt
