Healthy Homemade Ice Cream

Healthy vegan ice cream with no sugar added! No ice cream maker needed, either. 

1 ½ cups almond milk 
2  bananas - peeled, sliced, and frozen 
½ cup strawberries, sliced and frozen 
½ teaspoon vanilla extract
