Cheesecake Ice Cream

Great ice cream recipe, perfect with your favorite berries. 

1 quart lowfat 1% milk 
2 (8 ounce) packages reduced fat cream cheese, softened  
1 ½ cups white sugar 
⅓ cup triple sec 
1 tablespoon vanilla extract 
1 pinch salt
