Zucchini Pie III

This pie tastes like apple pie. 

2 (9 inch)  unbaked pie crusts 
4 cups zucchini, thinly sliced 
¾ cup white sugar 
2 tablespoons tapioca 
¼ cup lemon juice 
½ teaspoon salt 
2 teaspoons ground cinnamon 
½ teaspoon ground nutmeg 
2 teaspoons cornstarch 
2 teaspoons butter 
1 tablespoon milk
