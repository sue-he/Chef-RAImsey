Key Lime Thyme Pie

Key lime and a hint of thyme add indulgent flavor and intrigue to this fluffy no-bake pie. 

2 cups gluten-free graham crackers, blended 
¼ cup vegan butter, melted 
2 (11 ounce) bottles So Delicious® Culinary Coconut Milk 
2 cups powdered sugar 
1 pinch salt 
¼ teaspoon vanilla extract 
2 tablespoons organic cane sugar 
¾ cup key lime juice, or to taste 
1  lime, zested 
1 (9 ounce) tub So Delicious® Dairy Free CocoWhip 
  Candied thyme, for garnish (see directions)
