Burnt Caramel Pie

This is an old-fashioned pie.  It is very good, but rich! 

2 (9 inch)  pie shells, baked 
3 cups white sugar 
3 cups water 
4  eggs, separated 
1 cup evaporated milk 
½ cup light corn syrup 
¼ cup butter, melted 
½ teaspoon vanilla extract 
½ cup all-purpose flour 
½ teaspoon cream of tartar
