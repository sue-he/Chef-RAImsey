Pecan Pie

This is a wonderfully rich, Southern pie recipe that is the best I've tried! 

1 ¾ cups white sugar 
¼ cup dark corn syrup 
¼ cup butter 
1 tablespoon cold water 
2 teaspoons cornstarch 
3  eggs 
¼ teaspoon salt 
1 teaspoon vanilla extract 
1 ¼ cups chopped pecans 
1 (9 inch) unbaked pie shell
